# Nike High-Contrast

## Product Overview

**The Pitch:** LocalCheck brings the raw energy of street sports to your pocket. Find active courts, see who's playing, lock in your RSVP, and track your ELO ranking in real-time.

**For:** Competitive amateur athletes and weekend warriors who care about finding quality runs and tracking their reputation.

**Device:** mobile

**Design Direction:** Stark editorial brutalism. Heavy black-and-white layouts, massive typography for stats, and full-bleed community imagery sliced by rigid grids.

**Inspired by:** Nike SNKRS, Victory Journal

---

## Screens

- **Home / Map:** Full-screen map with brutalist court markers and live player counts.
- **Court Profile:** Editorial spread of court conditions, current check-ins, and upcoming runs.
- **Game Run:** Matchmaking lobby, team balancing, and RSVP status.
- **Player ELO:** Brutalist stat dashboard, recent matches, and ranking tier.
- **The Feed:** Reverse-chronological feed of action shots, check-ins, and court status updates.

---

## Key Flows

**Check into a Court:** Secure a spot and broadcast presence.

1. User is on **Home / Map** -> sees `3 ACTIVE` pulsing on "Rucker Park" marker.
2. User clicks marker -> Court bottom sheet slides up, displaying current players.
3. User clicks **"CHECK IN"** -> Status updates to "ON COURT", avatar added to live roster, feed event generated.

<summary>Design System</summary>

## Color Palette

- **Primary:** `#000000` - Absolute black for heavy buttons, active nav, primary text
- **Background:** `#FFFFFF` - Stark white for high contrast
- **Surface:** `#F4F4F4` - Light gray for secondary cards and inactive states
- **Text:** `#000000` - High legibility body
- **Muted:** `#888888` - Timestamps, borders, minor metadata
- **Accent:** `#DFFF00` - "Volt" green for live statuses, active toggles, and check-in buttons

## Typography

- **Headings:** `Oswald`, 700, 32-48px (All caps, tightly letter-spaced)
- **Body:** `Helvetica Neue`, 400, 16px
- **Small text:** `Helvetica Neue`, 500, 12px (All caps tracking)
- **Stats:** `Oswald`, 700, 64-96px (Brutalist data display)

**Style notes:** Hard edges (`0px` border radius). No drop shadows. 1px solid black borders to define grids. High-contrast monochromatic UI to let user-uploaded court photos pop.

<summary>Screen Specifications</summary>

### Home / Map

**Purpose:** Discover active games nearby geographically or via list view.

**Layout:** Full-bleed map interface with a floating top search bar and bottom navigation.

**Key Elements:**
- **Map Markers:** Black square badges with white text (e.g., `12 ON COURT`). Volt green dot if friends are present.
- **View Toggle:** `MAP / LIST` floating pill at top right. `MAP` is bold black, `LIST` is muted gray.
- **Search Bar:** Heavy 4px black bottom border, `SEARCH COURTS...` placeholder.

**States:**
- **Empty:** "NO COURTS IN RADIUS" brutalist overlay text.
- **Loading:** Map skeleton pulses between `#FFFFFF` and `#F4F4F4`.
- **Error:** "LOCATION DENIED" red banner at top.

**Components:**
- **Court Bottom Sheet:** Takes up 40% of screen height. Full bleed image header, `Oswald` H1 court name.

**Interactions:**
- **Click Marker:** Snaps camera to marker, pulls up bottom sheet with hard slide animation (`0.2s ease-out`).

### Court Profile

**Purpose:** Assess court vibe, view active players, and see upcoming scheduled runs.

**Layout:** Magazine spread style. Top 50% is a carousel of user-uploaded photos. Bottom 50% is scrolling content split by rigid 1px black lines.

**Key Elements:**
- **Header:** Overlaid text on photo. "VENICE BEACH", "BASKETBALL".
- **Live Roster:** Horizontal scroll of black-and-white player avatars.
- **Upcoming Runs:** List of scheduled games with `JOIN` button.
- **Action Button:** Sticky bottom full-width button. Volt green background, black text: `CHECK IN`.

**States:**
- **Empty:** No upcoming runs -> "BE THE FIRST TO HOST" CTA.

**Components:**
- **Run Card:** White background, 1px black border. Left side: Time (`18:00`). Right side: Host avatar and `4/10 PLAYERS`.

**Interactions:**
- **Click Check In:** Button turns black, text changes to `CHECKED IN`, emits brief haptic buzz.

### Game Run

**Purpose:** Lobby for a specific scheduled game. Handle RSVPs and team balancing.

**Layout:** Heavy typography header for time/location, followed by two side-by-side columns (Team A vs Team B).

**Key Elements:**
- **Title:** "5v5 FULL COURT"
- **Roster Split:** Left column `DARK`, Right column `LIGHT`.
- **Player Slots:** Empty slots say `OPEN` in gray. Filled slots show player name and ELO score.
- **Auto-Balance Toggle:** Switch to automatically shuffle teams based on ELO.

**States:**
- **Empty:** All slots show `OPEN`.
- **Error:** "LOBBY FULL" disabled state on join button.

**Components:**
- **Player Row:** 48px height. Avatar, Name, ELO (`1450`).

### Player ELO

**Purpose:** Public profile displaying reputation, rank, and recent match history.

**Layout:** Brutalist data dashboard. Massive numbers taking up significant vertical space.

**Key Elements:**
- **Rank Display:** 96px `Oswald` number: `1652`. Subtitle: `SILVER TIER`.
- **Win/Loss:** Giant text `W 42 / L 18`.
- **Recent Matches:** List of previous runs with `+15` or `-12` ELO adjustments in green/red.

**States:**
- **Empty:** "UNRANKED. PLAY 5 GAMES."
- **Loading:** Numbers scramble like an odometer before settling.

**Components:**
- **Match Row:** 1px black border top/bottom. Left: Date (`OCT 12`), Middle: Result (`WIN`), Right: Delta (`+12`).

### The Feed

**Purpose:** Chronological pulse of the local sports community.

**Layout:** Edge-to-edge vertical scroll of image-heavy cards.

**Key Elements:**
- **Feed Item:** Full-bleed image.
- **Metadata:** Black bar overlay at bottom of image. "MARCUS CHECKED INTO RUCKER PARK - 2 MINS AGO".
- **Hype Button:** Flame icon, count of hypes (`32`).

**States:**
- **Empty:** "NO ACTIVITY YET. GO PLAY."

**Components:**
- **Feed Card:** 100vw width, 400px height. Image cover.

<details>
<summary>Build Guide</summary>

**Stack:** HTML + Tailwind CSS v3

**Build Order:**
1. **Design System & Typography:** Set up `Oswald` and `Helvetica Neue`, establish sharp borders.
2. **Player ELO:** Simplest data-heavy screen to lock in the brutalist stat aesthetic.
3. **Court Profile:** Establishes the editorial image-heavy layout and grid lines.
4. **Game Run:** Implements the complex state management for rosters.
5. **Home / Map:** Complex map integration relies on finalized bottom sheet components.
6. **The Feed:** Endless scroll implementation.
